About Us

eBoB, founded in 2024 in Brussels, specializes in providing expert consultancy in Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC), with a strong focus on ISO 27001 implementation and IT risk assessments. eBoB combines agility with deep expertise to help organizations identify, evaluate, and mitigate cyber and IT risks effectively. Whether you're navigating complex regulatory frameworks, strengthening your information security management system, or seeking a pragmatic approach to risk assessment, eBoB is your trusted partner. With a commitment to precision, practicality, and trust, eBoB empowers businesses to confidently manage their digital risks and achieve compliance excellence.
